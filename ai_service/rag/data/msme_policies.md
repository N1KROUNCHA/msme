# Indian MSME Government Schemes Knowledge Base (2024-2025)

## 1. Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)
Launched in 2015, MUDRA provides collateral-free loans to micro and small enterprises.
- **Loan Categories:**
  - **Shishu:** Up to ₹50,000.
  - **Kishore:** ₹50,001 to ₹5 Lakh.
  - **Tarun:** ₹5 Lakh to ₹10 Lakh.
  - **Tarun Plus:** ₹10 Lakh to ₹20 Lakh (For successful Tarun repayers, introduced in 2024).
- **Eligibility:** Non-corporate, non-farm small/micro enterprises in manufacturing, trading, or services.
- **Key Update 2024:** Loan limit increased from ₹10 Lakh to ₹20 Lakh.

## 2. Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
A credit-linked subsidy scheme to generate self-employment.
- **Project Cost Limits:**
  - Manufacturing: Up to ₹50 Lakh.
  - Service Sector: Up to ₹20 Lakh.
- **Subsidy (Margin Money):** 15% to 35% based on category (General/Special) and location (Urban/Rural).
- **Special Categories:** Women, SC/ST, OBC, Ex-servicemen, Transgenders, Aspirational Districts, Hilly/Border areas.

## 3. Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)
Provides credit guarantees to lenders for collateral-free loans to MSEs.
- **Key Update 2025:** Maximum guarantee coverage increased from ₹5 Crore to ₹10 Crore, effective April 1, 2025.
- **Coverage:** 75% to 85% of the loan amount.
- **Guarantees Approved (FY 24-25):** Approximately ₹3 Lakh Crore.

## 4. MSME Samadhaan
A portal for addressing delayed payments to Micro and Small Enterprises.
- **Mechanism:** MSEs can file complaints against buyers for payments delayed beyond 45 days.
- **Interest:** Buyers must pay compound interest (3x of RBI bank rate) if payment is delayed.
- **Resolution:** Aims for resolution within 90 days via MSEFCs.

## 5. MSME Sambandh
A portal to monitor implementation of Public Procurement Policy.
- **Goal:** Ensures that 25% of annual procurement by Central Ministries/Departments/PSUs is from MSEs.
- **Market Access:** Provides detailed information on products being sourced by PSUs.

## 6. PM Vishwakarma Scheme
Supports traditional artisans and craftspeople.
- **Benefits:** Credit support (up to ₹3 Lakh in two tranches), toolkit incentive, skill training, and digital transaction incentives.
- **Interest Rate:** Concessional rate of 5%.
